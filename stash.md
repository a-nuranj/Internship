 STASH
  "Save my unfinished work temporarily"
  Real scenario:
    You're editing calc.py (half done)
    ↓
    Friend says "urgent! fix a bug in results.py"
    ↓
    You can't commit half-done work
    ↓
    Stash your changes → calc.py goes back to original
    ↓
    Fix results.py → commit → push
    ↓
    Restore your Stash → calc.py comes back with your half-done work!
